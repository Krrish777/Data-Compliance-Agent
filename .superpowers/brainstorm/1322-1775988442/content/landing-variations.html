II.
How a scan is conducted.
Act the First

The Policy is Read

A regulatory PDF is ingested, chunked, and processed. AI extracts structured compliance rules with confidence bands.

Act the Second

The Data is Scanned

Keyset-paginated queries sweep SQLite or Postgres tables. PII columns are detected via semantic similarity.

Act the Third

The Report is Issued

Violations are validated by LLM, explained in plain language, and compiled into PDF or HTML audit reports.

III.
The capabilities.
⚡

Dual-Model Strategy

70B for rule extraction and explanation. 8B for fast binary classification. Cost-optimized by design.

🛡️

SQL Interceptor

Real-time APPROVE/BLOCK verdicts on queries. Policy-grounded reasoning with complete audit trails.

🧑‍⚖️

Human Review Gate

Uncertain rules surface for human judgment. The compliance officer has the final word, always.
