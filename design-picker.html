Pipeline
Upload. Scan. Report.
1

Ingest Policy

PDF is chunked, embedded, and rules are extracted with confidence scores via Groq Llama 3.3-70B.

→
2

Scan Database

Keyset-paginated scans across SQLite or Postgres. PII detected via semantic similarity.

→
3

Generate Report

LLM-validated violations with explanations, severity grades, and remediation steps.

Capabilities
Everything you need for automated compliance
⚡

Dual-Model Strategy

70B for rule extraction and explanations. 8B for fast binary classification. Cost-optimized by default.

🔍

SQL Interceptor

Real-time APPROVE/BLOCK verdicts on queries before they touch your data. Policy-grounded with audit trails.

📊

Rich Audit Reports

PDF and HTML reports with violation details, compliance scores, and per-rule explanations.
